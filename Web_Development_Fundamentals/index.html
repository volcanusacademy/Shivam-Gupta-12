Legacy Of Discord Furious Wing

Legacy of Discord is an Asian MMO that closely follows the genre's standards but still has enough features (especially in the visuals department) to make it one of the absolute best games of its genre for Android devices. The characters and settings are much more detailed than what you can find in other games.
